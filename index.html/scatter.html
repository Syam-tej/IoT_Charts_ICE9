Optimized Flow Duration vs Packet Size (Outliers Removed)

This scatter (bubble) chart plots Flow_Duration on the x-axis and Packet_Size on the y-axis. Each point represents a single flow, colored by protocol (SMTP, FTP, SSH, HTTP, MQTT, etc.), while marker size reflects Packet_Count. Outliers were removed to focus on the main body of the data. The points are spread across a wide range of durations and packet sizes, indicating diverse traffic patterns across protocols rather than a single dominant cluster.
